DESCRIPTOIN OF THE FEATURES

	Distance: distance of the user to the nearest object

	Pressure: pressure on a cane for the elderly (2 for high and 0 for small)
	
	HRV: Heart rate variability

	Sugar level: level of sugar in the blood

	SpO2: oxygen saturation

	Accelerometer: Acceleration of the person

	Decision: 2 for fall, 1 for slipped/tripped and 0 for no fall
